### Curve1

- **Type:** Wire
- **Required:** Yes
- **Description:** First curve

### Curve2

- **Type:** Wire
- **Required:** Yes
- **Description:** Second curve

## Outputs

### Surface

- **Type:** Face
- **Description:** Ruled surface
